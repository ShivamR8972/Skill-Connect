You can browse jobs on the "Browse Jobs" page. Click on any job to see details and use the "Apply Now" button. This will open a form where you can write an optional cover letter and submit your application.

When you apply for a job, recruiters can see the information you've added to your profile, such as your skills, experience, and resume. Keeping your profile complete and up-to-date is the best way to stand out.
